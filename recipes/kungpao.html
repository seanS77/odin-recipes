Kung Pao Chicken

Description

Kung Pao chicken is a spicy, stir fried Chinese meal. It is made of cubes of chicken, peanuts, vegatables, and chili peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp white wine
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp corn starch dissolved in 2 tbsp of water
  • 1 oz chili paste
  • 1 tsp distilled white vinegar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 4 chopped green onions
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 8 oz water chestnuts
  • 4 oz chopped peanuts

Steps

  1. Combine 1 tbsp wine, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp oil and 1 tbsp cornstarch/water mixture and mix together. Toss chicken in bowl with marinade. Cover bowl and refrigerate for 30 min.
  2. Combine 1 tbsp wine, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp oil, 1 tbsp cornstarch/water mixture, chili paste, vinegar and sugar in a bowl. Mix together and add green onion, garlic, water chestnuts and peanuts. Slowly heat sauce in a skillet.
  3. Remove chicken from marinade and saute in a large skillet until meat is white and juices run clear. Add sauteed chicken to it and let simmer together until sauce thickens.
