Pancakes

Description

An easy breakfast recipe that makes enough for 4 people.

Items

  • 1 c all purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp white suger
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 c milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, baking powder, sugar and salt in a bowl. Pour in milk, egg and oil and mix well.
  2. Heat lightly oiled griddle/pan over medium high heat. Pour approx. 1/4 c batter on pan for each pancake. Brown on both sides.
