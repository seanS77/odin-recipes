Strawberry Pie

Description

Easy to make pie with strawberry filling and a sweet crust. Recipe yields one 9 in pie.

Items

  • 1 9 in baked pie crust
  • 1 qt fresh strawberries
  • 1 c white sugar
  • 3 tbsp starch
  • 3/4 c water
  • 1/2 whipping cream

Steps

  1. Arrange half of strawberries within the pie crust. Mash remaining berries and mix with sugar in saucepan. Boil over medium height, stir frequently.
  2. Whisk together starch and warer in a bowl. Gradually stir starch mixture into boiling berry mixture. Reduce heat and simmer until thickened, stir constantly. Pour mixture over berries in pie crust.
  3. Chill for several hours. Serve pie with dollop of whipped cream.
